About me!

I have academic education in laser and optics sciences, and experimental research experiences in different laser spectroscopy techniques, mostly focused on Raman spectroscopy, pomp-probe spectroscopy and ultrafast spectroscopy. I have expertise in optical designing, UV-VIS absorption, FTIR, optomechanical devices and chemical kinetics. My recent project was on tracking vibrational control of thermally activated reactions by using ultrafast time- resolved Raman spectroscopy.
